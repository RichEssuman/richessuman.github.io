Mission Statements


The mission of the School is to pursue and maintain standards among school-going age.

It is the aim of the school to produce young men and women who are independent

thinkers with physical and moral courage, who are articulate, well mannered and tolerant,

educated in the widest sense of the word, sensitive to the needs of others, and dedicated

to the service of their fellow men and women and the community as a whole.

We are a private institution that offers quality education.
